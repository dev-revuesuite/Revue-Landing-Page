Frequently Asked Questions About Quality Control Tools for Creative Agencies

What is Revue's quality control tool? ▼

Revue is an automated quality control platform designed specifically for creative agencies. It uses proprietary AI technology to automatically detect spelling errors, alignment inconsistencies, word spacing issues, line-height problems, and pixel-level defects in static design files. Unlike manual QC processes, Revue catches errors consistently and objectively, reducing revision cycles and improving delivery quality.

How does Revue's proprietary QC engine work? ▼

Revue's proprietary QC engine analyzes design files at the pixel level using advanced algorithms trained on thousands of design files and quality issues. When you upload a design, the engine automatically scans for 50+ common errors including spelling mistakes, misaligned elements, inconsistent spacing, typography issues, and color problems. It then generates a detailed report highlighting each defect with exact location, severity level, and recommended fixes.

Is Revue better than manual quality control? ▼

Yes, significantly. Manual quality control has several limitations: human reviewers miss 10-15% of defects even under the best conditions, it consumes 15-20 hours per week of expensive team resources, and quality varies depending on reviewer fatigue and attention. Revue's automated approach achieves 95%+ defect detection rates, works instantly regardless of file complexity, and provides completely consistent quality standards. Most agencies see 80% reduction in QC time when they switch to Revue.

How does Revue compare to Ziflow and GoVisually? ▼

Ziflow and GoVisually are client proofing and feedback collaboration platforms. They're designed for client review and approval workflows, not automated quality control detection. Revue is fundamentally different—it's built specifically for QC teams to catch design errors before client review. Revue detects pixel-level defects automatically, while Ziflow and GoVisually rely on client comments and manual review. For agencies focused on error prevention and quality assurance, Revue is the right tool.

What file formats does Revue support? ▼

Revue supports all major design file formats including Adobe Creative Suite files (PSD, AI, PDF), image files (PNG, JPG, GIF, SVG), and other static design formats. You can upload individual files or batch multiple files for comprehensive project-wide QC analysis.

Can I customize Revue's QC standards for my agency? ▼

Absolutely. Revue allows you to define custom QC standards based on your agency's specific brand guidelines and quality requirements. Set rules for spacing, typography, color consistency, and more. Revue learns your standards and applies them consistently across all QC scans.

How quickly does Revue generate QC reports? ▼

Revue generates detailed QC reports within seconds of upload, regardless of file size or complexity. This means you get instant feedback and can fix issues immediately, eliminating wait times and accelerating your design delivery cycle.

Is there a learning curve with Revue? ▼

No. Revue is designed for immediate usability. You can be up and running in under 5 minutes. Simply upload a design file, and Revue does the rest. The intuitive dashboard shows all detected issues with clear explanations and fixes.

Does Revue integrate with existing design workflows? ▼

Yes. Revue integrates with popular design tools and project management platforms, allowing you to incorporate QC checks directly into your existing workflow without disruption. This means seamless adoption and minimal training required.

What industries and design types does Revue serve? ▼

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Revue (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates revuesuite.com and related products for creative agencies and design teams. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website, waitlist, blog, or software.

Information we collect

  • Account and waitlist details — name, email address, company or studio name, and plan preferences when you join the beta or reserve early access.
  • Payment information — payments are processed by Razorpay. We do not store full card details on our servers; we may receive payment status, transaction IDs, and billing metadata.
  • Usage and product data — when you use Revue, we may collect log data, device/browser information, and in-product activity needed to operate and improve the service.
  • Communications — messages you send us (including email to admin@revuesuite.com) and support correspondence.
  • Analytics — we use tools such as Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity to understand site usage. These services may use cookies or similar technologies.

How we use your information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve Revue and our website
  • Process waitlist sign-ups, early access reservations, and payments
  • Send product updates, onboarding information, and service-related messages
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Monitor security, prevent fraud, and enforce our Terms
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

Legal basis (where applicable)

We process personal data based on your consent (for example, marketing sign-ups), performance of a contract (providing the service you requested), and our legitimate interests in operating and improving Revue.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (hosting, email, analytics, payment processing) who assist us under confidentiality obligations
  • Professional advisers or authorities when required by law
  • A successor entity in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale

Data retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service, meet legal requirements, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Waitlist and account data may be kept until you ask us to delete it, subject to legal limits.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your personal data, or to withdraw consent where processing is consent-based. To make a request, email admin@revuesuite.com.

Cookies

Our site may use cookies and similar technologies for functionality, analytics, and preferences. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling cookies may affect some features.

Third-party links

Our website may link to third-party sites (for example, social networks or payment providers). Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

Children

Revue is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will reflect changes. Continued use of our services after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
